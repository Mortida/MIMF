# Ethical Use Guidelines for MIMF

MIMF (Metadata Intelligence & Mutation Framework) is a powerful toolkit for inspecting, analyzing, and safely mutating metadata across files and data structures. With great power comes responsibility.  

## Guidelines

1. **Legal Compliance**  
   - Only use MIMF on files and data you own or have explicit permission to access and modify.  
   - Do not use MIMF to tamper with or exploit other users' data.  

2. **Privacy & Security**  
   - Do not process sensitive, personal, or confidential data without proper authorization.  
   - Take precautions to prevent accidental data leaks.  

3. **Ethical Research & Development**  
   - MIMF is intended for legitimate research, security testing, data management, and development purposes.  
   - Avoid using MIMF for malicious activities, including hacking, data corruption, or evasion.  

4. **Responsible Sharing**  
   - If sharing modified files or results, ensure no sensitive information is included.  
   - Always cite and credit MIMF when using it in academic or professional work.  

## Disclaimer

The developers of MIMF are **not responsible for misuse or damage caused by this software**. By using MIMF, you agree to comply with ethical and legal guidelines.
